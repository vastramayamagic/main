Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 14 January 2025

Welcome to Vastramayamagic.in ("we", "our", "us").

Vastramayamagic.in is an e-commerce platform offering traditional and vintage Indian clothing across India. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, stored, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you place an order, contact us, or interact with our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Order details and purchase history
Important: We do not store your credit/debit card numbers, UPI IDs, PINs, or banking credentials.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you browse our website, limited technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages viewed and time spent on the website

This data is used only for basic website functionality and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Delivering products within India
  • Communicating order confirmations, shipping updates, and customer support
  • Managing returns, refunds, or replacements
  • Preventing fraud, misuse, or unauthorized transactions
  • Complying with applicable Indian laws and regulations

3. Cookies

Vastramayamagic.in uses essential cookies only to:

  • Maintain website functionality
  • Enable secure checkout
  • Improve user experience

We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features of the website may not function properly.

4. Payment Processing

All payments on Vastramayamagic.in are processed securely through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.

Razorpay may collect and process your payment information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or store your sensitive payment details.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Razorpay for payment processing
  • Logistics and delivery partners for order fulfillment
  • Legal or government authorities if required under Indian law

All parties involved are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete transactions
  • Maintain business and legal records
  • Resolve disputes or customer issues

Once no longer required, the data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Secure servers
  • Restricted internal access

However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

Vastramayamagic.in does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If such data is identified, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated "Last Updated" date.

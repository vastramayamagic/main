01 General Information

Vastramaya offers a curated collection of traditional Indian clothing, bridging timeless handloom heritage with modern elegance. Each piece is sourced directly from trusted artisans across India, ensuring authenticity in every thread.
We capture our products in studio lighting to be as true to life as possible. However, slight variations may occur due to different screen calibrations or the handcrafted nature of natural dyes. These nuances are the hallmark of authentic ethnic wear.

03 Shipping & Delivery

Currently, we ship exclusively within India. We are working on expanding our logistics to serve global customers soon.
Standard processing takes 1–2 business days. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location (Metro cities are faster). You will receive tracking details via email/SMS once dispatched.
Please do not accept a package if the seal is broken or the box is visibly damaged. If you discover damage after opening, please record an unboxing video and email us within 48 hours for a swift resolution.

04 Returns & Refunds

We accept returns for damaged, defective, or incorrect items reported within 48 hours of delivery. The product must be unused, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.
Clearance or Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged. Please check the product description carefully before purchasing.
